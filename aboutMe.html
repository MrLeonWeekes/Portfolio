About Lee

I am a Junior Web Developer with a love and passion for coding and gained a certification in Web Development from the training provider Just IT in the last year. Within this course we learnt HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, MongoDB & MySQL. This has has been instrumental in allowing me to develop interactive and responsive games, platforms and websites to showcase my talents.

Also I have a strong knowledge of Content Management Systems such as Wordpress & Squarespace which are very important and robust tools in designing and building websites.

I enjoy completing coding challenges and have a clear, logical mind with a practical approach to problem-solving and a drive to see things through to completion. My career aim is to gain a Junior Developer role which allows me to further my new skills and collaborate with team members and other teams to perform the tasks effectively. If given an opportunity, I can readily translate my skills and expertise into actionable outcomes for the benefit of your organization.

Outside of coding I am a lover of physical fitness and mental wellbeing. I meditate, coach junior football, train in the gym, swim (slowly) and play badminton.
